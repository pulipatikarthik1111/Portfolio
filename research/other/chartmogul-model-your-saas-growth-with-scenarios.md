# ChartMogul — Model your SaaS growth with Scenarios (summary)

Source: `https://chartmogul.com/blog/model-your-saas-growth-with-scenarios/`  
Collected: 2026-04-15  
Last updated (article): 2025-08-14

## Executive summary

ChartMogul’s **Scenarios** feature is a “what if” modeling tool that projects **ARR** and **subscriber count** based on recent baseline performance and adjustable growth levers (acquisition, pricing, churn). It’s designed for pressure-testing plans and aligning teams around realistic targets.

## What Scenarios does

- Uses historical performance as baseline (choose last **3, 6, or 9 months**).
- Lets you model changes in common SaaS levers:
  - **New subscriber volume**
  - **Pricing changes**
  - **Customer churn rate**
- Each lever can be increased/decreased by % and applied immediately or over time.
- Supports combining multiple conditions.
- Shows baseline vs scenario projections (dynamic chart updates).

## Example questions this supports

- If we reduce churn by X%, what happens to ARR in 12 months?
- What is the ARR impact of a price increase of Y%?
- If trial volume doubles, what happens to subscribers/ARR?

## Practical workflow (as described)

1. Go to Scenarios in ChartMogul (`https://app.chartmogul.com/#/scenarios`)
2. Choose baseline window (3/6/9 months)
3. Add “what-if” conditions (subscriber volume, pricing, churn)
4. Review month/quarter/year projections (baseline vs scenario)

## How to use this in GTM planning (notes)

- Pair churn + pricing scenarios to understand net revenue impact.
- Use scenario projections to set:
  - pipeline targets
  - retention goals
  - pricing experimentation guardrails
